Diagnostic Summary

TRADITIONAL SEARCH operates by checking every single item from the start until it hits the target. Its complexity scales exactly with the size of the data.

BINARY SEARCH is significantly more advanced. By assuming the data is sorted, it can eliminate 50% of the entire search space in every single cycle.

The Scale Advantage

On this small dataset, the difference is measured in cycles. On a dataset of 4 billion items, Traditional search might take 4 billion steps, while Binary search will finish in at most 32 steps.
